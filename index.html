Audio Restoration

Breathing New Life Into Damaged Audio

Not all audio damage is the same, and neither are our solutions. Avatar specialises in both subtle enhancement and deep forensic restoration, applying decades of hands-on experience to film archive audio restoration, audio preservation and broadcast legacy material.

We are the audio consultant to the Irish Film Institute Film Archive Department, trusted with the preservation of Ireland's most historically significant soundtracks.

  • Click, pop, crackle & hiss removal
  • Spectral repair of damaged recordings
  • Optical & magnetic soundtrack restoration
  • Dialogue noise reduction & cleanup
  • IASA archival standard delivery
  • All legacy formats supported

About

Killian FitzGerald

Killian is one of Ireland's most experienced audio post-production specialists, with a career spanning 35 years across Dublin, London, and New York.

He began at Tommy Ellis Sound Studios in 1989, then held senior positions at Carlton Television (Head of Audio Post Production), Space Facilities, Molinaire, and Oasis TV in London, working for BBC, Channel 4, and ITV.

In 2007 he founded Avatar Audio Post Production. He lectures at Pulse College and Griffith College.
